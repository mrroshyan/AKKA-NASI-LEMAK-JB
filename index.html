About Akka

Founded by Malika Balakrishnan and her husband Perabu Ramachandran, Akka Nasi Lemak JB is a hawker stall dedicated to the early‑morning rhythm of Johor Bahru. We open at dawn to serve pandan‑infused nasi lemak wrapped in kertas bungkus — fragrant rice, signature sambal, crunchy ikan bilis and peanuts, egg, and classic toppings made with care.

Our hours are short because freshness matters. It’s fuel for the day — especially for those crossing into Singapore for work — and a taste of home rooted in tradition.
